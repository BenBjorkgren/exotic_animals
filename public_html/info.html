How to take care of a Zebra:

  1. Brush it everyday.
  2. Make sure it has plenty of grass. You can also feed it carrots.
  3. Zebras are prone to disease. Make sure you know a vet.

How to take care of a Spider Monkey:

  1. Provide it with many trees and vegetation.
  2. Feed it fruits and veggies.
  3. They tend to make a mess with everything they do. Be prepared to clean.

How to take care of a Crocodile:

  1. Make sure it has plenty of water to swim in.
  2. Feed it fish.

How to take care of a Bear:

  1. They need a lot of exercise. Make sure to take them on walks everyday.
  2. Feed him salmon.
  3. Don't be alarmed in the winter- it's just hibernating
